Welcome!

I study carbon and nitrogen cycling in rivers and oceans as well as the microbial life that powers these cycles. Currently, I am a Gund Fellow at the University of Vermont Rubenstein School of Environment and Natural Resources, advised by Carol Adair. I got my PhD in December 2021 in Rick Keil's group at the University of Washington School of Oceanography in Seattle.
On this site you can find my CV, publications, and more about my research.
